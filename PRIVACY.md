# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 4, 2026  
**App:** atmos  
**Developer:** Zakhary Kipp

---

## Overview

atmos is a weather app for iPhone. This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used. The short version: atmos does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

---

## Information We Access

### Location
atmos requests access to your device's location solely to retrieve local weather conditions and forecasts. Your location is sent directly to Apple's WeatherKit service to fetch weather data. It is never transmitted to our servers, logged, or stored.

Location access is optional. If you deny permission, atmos will not be able to display weather for your current location.

### No Other Data
atmos does not access, collect, or store:
- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Photos, contacts, or any other device data
- Browsing history or app usage behavior
- Device identifiers or advertising identifiers

---

## Third-Party Services

atmos uses **Apple WeatherKit** to provide weather data. WeatherKit is an Apple service and is subject to Apple's own privacy policy, available at [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

---

## Data Retention

We do not retain any user data because we do not collect any.

---

## Children's Privacy

atmos does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no features that require personal data from any user, regardless of age.
